About Me

  • Background

    My decision to pursue web development began in 2020 during the height of the Coronavirus pandemic. I am currently a massage therapist, so, consequently, my career was put on hold until I got vaccinated. I realized I needed to seek a profession that provided me stability, but also something to satisfy my itch to learn something new. I brainstormed a few routes I could take that would fit my needs, and after testing a couple, I landed at Epicodus. I feel confident that my peers and I will enjoy and ultimately succeed in our chosen career paths!
